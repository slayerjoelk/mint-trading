```
class RegimeClassifier:
    REGIMES = ['trending_up', 'trending_down', 'ranging', 'high_volatility', 'low_volatility', 'crisis']

        correlation of SP500 sectors, consecutive up/down days, drawdown from peak."""

```

Classification logic (statistical, not trained ML — sklearn/scikit-learn may not be available):
- Score each regime using weighted feature thresholds
- trending_up: SPY > 20SMA > 50SMA, low VIXY, positive momentum, IWM/SPY ratio stable
- trending_down: SPY < 20SMA < 50SMA, rising VIXY, negative momentum, drawdown > 5%
- ranging: SPY near 20SMA (within ±2%), medium VIX, low momentum magnitude
- high_volatility: VIXY elevated (>$20 or 50% above 20-day low), ATR/price > 2%
- low_volatility: VIXY compressed (<$15), ATR/price < 1%, tight Bollinger Bands
- crisis: extreme conditions — VIXY > $25-30, SPY -10%+ from peak, high correlation

Store regime classifications in DB table: `regime_history` with date, regime, confidence, features (JSON).
