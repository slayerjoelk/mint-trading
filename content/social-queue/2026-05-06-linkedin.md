# LinkedIn Post — "The Invisible Tax on Verbal Agreements"

Every freelancer and agency owner pays a hidden tax:

The "we'll sort the paperwork later" tax.

You agree to scope in a call. The client is excited. You start work. Two weeks in, they "remember" something different. Suddenly you're doing 40% more work for the same price.

That's scope creep. And it's not the client's fault — it's the system's fault.

---

## Why Traditional Contracts Fail at the Speed of Modern Work

The average freelance project begins in a DM, a WhatsApp thread, or a 15-minute voice note.

Then someone says: *"I'll send the contract over."*

And the energy dies.

- The PDF sits unread for 3 days.
- The client "forgets" to sign.
- Work starts anyway, on a handshake.
- Misalignment shows up two weeks later.

By then, you're arguing over scope instead of shipping value.

## What If Sign-Off Happened in 90 Seconds?

At ClarAccord, we built something different:

**Conversation → Structured Receipt → OTP Sign-Off.**

No printing. No scanning. No DocuSign login they forgot the password to.

## The Real Benefit Isn't Speed — It's Alignment

When both parties see the scope in plain language *before* work starts, there's nowhere for ambiguity to hide.

If the scope changes (and it will), you don't argue. You reference the signed receipt and propose a revision. Professional. Calm. Profitable.
