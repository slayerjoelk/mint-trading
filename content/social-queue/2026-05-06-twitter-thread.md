# Twitter Thread — "The 90-Second Sign-Off"

## Tweet 1 (Hook)
The biggest lie in freelancing:

"We'll sort the contract later."

Later = never.
Later = scope creep.
Later = you eat the cost.

Here's how to get sign-off in 90 seconds — while the client is still excited. 🧵

## Tweet 2
Step 1: Stop writing contracts.

A 5-page PDF is friction.
Friction kills momentum.

Instead, summarize the scope in 3 bullets right in the chat:
→ Deliverable
→ Deadline
→ Price

That's your receipt. Not a novel.

## Tweet 3
Step 2: Make saying "yes" stupidly easy.

Don't ask them to print, sign, scan, and email back.

Send a link. They tap. OTP arrives. They confirm.

90 seconds. Signed. Timestamped. Done.

## Tweet 4
Step 3: Turn any conversation into a receipt.

Voice note on WhatsApp? 
Slack thread with 47 messages?
DM that went back and forth?

All of it becomes a structured, signed record.

No more "I thought you said…"

## Tweet 5
This isn't just faster.
It's *safer*.

Because when the scope creeps (and it will), you pull up the signed receipt.

"Here's exactly what we agreed. Happy to add the new scope — here's the revised price."

Professional. Calm. Profitable.
